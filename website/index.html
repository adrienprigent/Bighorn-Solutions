How We Work

Our Approach

A straightforward, collaborative process designed to deliver measurable results without unnecessary complexity.

01

Discover

We start by understanding your business inside and out. We review your current tools, workflows, and pain points to build a complete picture of where AI and automation can make the biggest impact.

02

Design

We map out a practical solution tailored to your specific needs. No over-engineered systems or unnecessary complexity. Just clear, focused solutions that solve real problems.

03

Build

We implement the solution, integrating with your existing tools and systems wherever possible. We build in stages so you see progress and can provide feedback along the way.

04

Support

After launch, we train your team and provide ongoing support to make sure everything runs smoothly. We stay available to optimize, adjust, and scale your systems as your business grows.

About Us

A Bow Valley Consultant Who Knows Your World.

Bighorn Solutions is based in Exshaw, Alberta — right in the heart of the Bow Valley. We were founded on a simple conviction: the AI and automation advantages that large enterprises enjoy should be accessible to the small and medium businesses that make this valley run.

Our founder brings a background in operations management at Rocky Mountain Soap Company — hands-on leadership across supply chain, team management, and multi-function operations. Combined with active service as a firefighter with Bighorn Fire Department, where high-stakes decisions, clear communication under pressure, and protecting what matters are the job every single day.

That combination shapes how we work: understand the real problem first, then build something practical that works. We’re not a remote agency parachuting in from Calgary. We live here. We know your seasons, your staffing pressures, and what it actually takes to run a business in this valley.
